Husman Moderna

Husman Moderna opened its doors in 2024, nestled in the heart of SÃ¶dermalm, Stockholm. Here, we embrace the essence of traditional Swedish cuisine, reimagined for today's palate. We believe that the culinary habits of Swedes have evolved beyond traditional dishes like lapskojs, palt, or blodpudding. Instead, we focus on meals that find their way into Swedish homes more frequently today. Dishes such as Bolognese, Stroganoff, and Warm-smoked Salmon are prepared with finesse, offering inspired versions that still nod to their roots. Alongside these, we also delight in serving Swedish classics like Toast Skagen and Sotare, ensuring a menu that's both familiar and intriguing.

At Husman Moderna, our philosophy is simple: to offer a dining experience that feels like an extension of your own home, yet transports you with each bite. Our inviting venue is designed to be a sanctuary where comfort meets sophistication. The interiors blend rustic charm with contemporary elegance, creating an atmosphere that encourages conviviality and leisurely dining.

Our commitment to quality is unwavering. We source our ingredients from local farmers and producers, ensuring that every dish not only supports our community but is of the highest standard. Our menu is a testament to our belief in the power of simple ingredients to create extraordinary meals.

As the world becomes increasingly interconnected, our menu reflects a blend of local and international influences, acknowledging the diverse culinary landscape of modern Sweden. Our chefs are artisans who respect the heritage of Swedish cuisine while embracing global techniques and flavors. This fusion is at the heart of what makes Husman Moderna unique.

We invite you to join us at Husman Moderna, where every visit is an opportunity to explore Swedish cuisine redefined. Whether it's a casual lunch, a family dinner, or a special celebration, our team is dedicated to making your experience memorable. Step into our world, where the warmth of Swedish hospitality meets the excitement of contemporary cuisine. Welcome to Husman Moderna, where tradition and innovation dine together.
